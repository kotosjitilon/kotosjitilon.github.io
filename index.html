Welcome to Authentic Village Life

Step into our authentically restored heritage home and immerse yourself in Sabah's rich Kadazan culture. Located in the serene Kampung Kuai, our homestay offers a unique gateway to authentic village life, surrounded by nature's beauty and the gentle flow of the Moyog River.

Experience the perfect blend of traditional wooden architecture, bamboo flooring, and modern amenities. Every corner tells a story of heritage, hospitality, and harmony with nature.
